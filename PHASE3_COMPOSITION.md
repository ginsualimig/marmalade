# Phase 3 Composition Rationale

- **Singular focal point:** The new battle stage gives the Keeper arena full real estate, with a generous boss frame, glow, and HUD that keep the boss as the visual hero and centre of gravity.
- **Player as guest:** The player sprite now lives in a compact, grounded strip below the boss so it feels like the hero visits the Keeper&apos;s domain rather than competing for attention.
- **Question overlay as focus:** The timed question card now floats over the stage, softening the background with a dim blur and carrying the skill/power headers, timer, options, and wrong-answer feedback. It never displaces the stage so the battle flow stays intact while the question becomes the only interactive focus during each prompt.
- **Preserved systems:** No mechanics were simplified; HP, damage, combo badges, particles, timers, and pedagogy overlays all remain unchanged under the new hierarchy.
