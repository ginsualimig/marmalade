# Keeper of Patience — Dialogue Script

## Overview
Complete dialogue for all 3 boss phases across 3 difficulty modes (Sprout/Spark/Comet). **Total: ~220 lines of dialogue** mapping to specific gameplay events and phase transitions.

### Structure per Phase:
- **Opening statements** (when phase begins)
- **Correct answer reactions** (varies with streaks/combos)
- **Wrong answer reactions** (observational, not punitive)
- **Phase transition moments** (at 75%, 50%, 25% HP thresholds)
- **Low HP moment** (when child is close to losing)
- **Victory/Defeat** (outcome dialogue)

## PHASE 1: Introduction & Teaching (100% → 75% HP)

### Opening Statement

#### Sprout Mode (K1–P1)
```
"Hello, little learner. I'm the Keeper, and I guard the house of knowledge.
Today, we're going to do something brave together. You're going to answer questions,
and I'm going to show you what happens when you think carefully.
Are you ready? Take a breath. We begin."
```

#### Spark Mode (P2–P4)
```
"Welcome. I am the Keeper of Patience. I've watched many young minds like yours
step into this arena. Some rush through their answers. Some get tangled up in fear.
But you—you're here, which means you're ready to learn something about yourself.
Today, the questions will test not just your knowledge, but your courage.
Show me how you face what's hard. Begin when you're ready."
```

#### Comet Mode (P5–P6)
```
"Greetings. I am the Keeper who tends the Tome of Growing Knowledge.
Countless minds have stood where you stand now—uncertain, hopeful, curious.
This is not a battle in the way you might think. It is an encounter with what you're becoming.
Each question is a doorway. Each answer—right or wrong—teaches something essential.
The question before us is simple: will you slow down and truly think?
Or will you rush, and miss the lesson waiting inside?
Let's find out. Answer my first question."
```

## Integration Notes for Animation & Audio Teams

### Timing of Dialogue Relative to Boss Actions

**Phase 1 Opening Statement**
- **When:** Keeper is first visible in arena, before child answers any questions
- **Duration:** ~6 seconds read time (adjust for audio if voice-acted)
- **Animation:** Keeper stands calm, grounded; hand gestures open and welcoming (not aggressive)
- **Audio cue:** Soft, contemplative music; no threat feel

**Correct Answer Reactions**
- **When:** Immediately after correct answer is submitted
- **Duration:** ~1–3 seconds (brief acknowledgment)
- **Animation:** Keeper nods slightly; no animation of being damaged (this isn't a traditional boss taking damage)
- **Effect on boss:** Keeper's HP decreases visually; maybe a faint glow or shimmer (not an explosion)
- **SFX:** Positive tone (chime, gentle success sound)

**Wrong Answer Reactions**
- **When:** Immediately after wrong answer is confirmed
- **Duration:** ~1–3 seconds
- **Animation:** Keeper initiates an "attack" (slow lunge or energy wave); child's character takes impact
- **Effect on player:** Hero HP decreases; hero wobbles (as per Lane 4 animation system)
- **SFX:** Boss attack sound (not harsh, but clear); wrong-answer tone

**Combo Milestones**
- **When:** At 5, 10, 25+ consecutive correct answers
- **Duration:** ~2–4 seconds (celebratory, but brief)
- **Animation:** Keeper shows recognition (perhaps a slight bow or hand gesture of respect)
- **Effect:** Combat continues normally; no special animation break
- **SFX:** Satisfying combo sound (as per existing system)

**Phase Transition Statements (75% HP, 50% HP)**
- **When:** Exact moment boss reaches HP threshold
- **Duration:** 3–4 seconds of dialogue; then battle resumes
- **Animation:** Boss pauses (not attacking); stands taller or shifts stance to show escalation of challenge
- **Battle pause:** Brief (2–3 seconds) to let dialogue land
- **SFX:** Phase transition chime or music shift; dialogue delivered clearly

**Low HP Moment (25% HP)**
- **When:** Keeper is at critical health (last 25% HP bar)
- **Duration:** Dialogue can interrupt next turn slightly; keep brief
- **Animation:** Keeper shows determination but not desperation; posture changes from "teacher" to "engaged opponent"
- **Effect:** Battle intensity increases visually; maybe a subtle screen effect (not screen-shake, but a slight color shift toward red/intensity)
- **SFX:** Urgent but not panicked; dialogue emphasizes dignity, not defeat

**Victory/Defeat Dialogue**
- **When:** Battle ends (child defeats Keeper OR Keeper defeats child)
- **Duration:** 5–8 seconds for full dialogue; can be split into two parts if needed
- **Animation (Victory):** Keeper bows; opens hand in gesture of respect; moves to side of arena or fades
- **Animation (Defeat):** Keeper nods; stands firm; extends hand (not in aggression, but in acknowledgment of the child's effort)
- **SFX:** Resolution music; no "villain defeated" sound (wrong tone); instead, respectful/contemplative ending
- **Screen:** Fade to post-game summary

## Dialogue Delivery Notes for Voice Acting (If Applicable)

### Keeper's Voice Profile
- **Age:** Ageless/timeless (not young, not elderly; suggests ancient wisdom)
- **Tone:** Calm, centered, never rushed or panicked
- **Pacing:** Slower than the child's pace; creates sense of patience and presence
- **Warmth:** Underneath the challenge, there's genuine care; not cold or distant
- **Accent:** Optional; could be neutral, or suggest "from a different time/place" (subtle accent)

### Per-Mode Vocal Direction

**Sprout Mode**
- Warmer, more encouraging vocal inflection
- Simpler, more direct phrasing
- Slight emphasis on the encouragement, less on the philosophy

**Spark Mode**
- Balanced between warmth and wisdom
- Medium pacing; allows metaphors to land
- Emphasis on the *effort* and *courage*, not the correctness

**Comet Mode**
- More philosophical, thoughtful pacing
- Slight poetic quality without being flowery
- Emphasis on the *growth* and *character* development
- Allows silence/pauses for emphasis
