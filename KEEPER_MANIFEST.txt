QUALITY GATES VALIDATION
================================================================================

✅ KIDS CARE ABOUT THE KEEPER
   • Distinct personality with clear motivations
   • Treats child with genuine respect and recognition
   • Dialogue emphasizes child's character and growth, not just scores
   • Visual design (wise mentor, calm presence) supports emotional connection

✅ DIALOGUE IS WARM (NEVER CONDESCENDING)
   • No generic affirmations ("You're amazing!" ❌ "Notice how you paused?" ✅)
   • Specific feedback tied to this child's actual actions
   • Age-appropriate language per difficulty mode
   • Treats children as capable people, not toddlers

✅ EMOTIONALLY RESONANT
   • Cutscenes create moments of genuine self-reflection
   • Dialogue arc progresses: "Test" → "Growth" → "Character"
   • Even defeat feels like growth opportunity, not failure
   • Victory feels earned because it emphasizes effort

✅ TIES TO GAMEPLAY
   • Dialogue responds to: combos, wrong answers, phase transitions, low HP
   • Keeper acknowledges child's actual performance in this run
   • Narrative reinforces learning-through-challenge, not punishment
   • Game mechanics (3 phases, timer, combos) integrated into story

================================================================================
CORE PHILOSOPHY
================================================================================

The Keeper of Patience teaches children that:

1. Patience is power
   → Slow thinking beats rushed answers

2. Mistakes are teachers
   → Every wrong answer shows where to grow

3. Character matters more than scores
   → Trying when things are hard is real strength

4. You're capable of more than you believe
   → Growth comes when you keep going despite doubt

Every line of dialogue, every phase transition, every cutscene reinforces one or
more of these truths.
